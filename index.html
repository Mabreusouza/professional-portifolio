    Artist Statement

    Wavy, colorful, and warm. That's how I would describe my artwork. Sometimes more light-hearted and sweet, sometimes more reflective and personal, but always wavy. Why wavy? Curvy lines, bubbly shapes, and wavy hatchures create a sense of fluidiness, softness, and movement. I like the changeable and irregular nature of wavy lines in comparison to more sharp geometric shapes. Being in the ocean, wind, trees, fire, clouds, plants, hair, our clothes, or even in the shape of our muscles and skin. I seek to evoke the smooth gestures and signs present all around us. But using unrealistic vibrant colors that reflect my personal emotions and what I believe brings comfort. Like the golden yellow shimmering light from a window, or the turquoise colors of the water on a river! That’s what I want to capture with my work.
